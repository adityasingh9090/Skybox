About Us

Welcome to Sky Box Cricket Turf

Sky Box Cricket Turf is a premium box cricket facility located in Purnea, Bihar. We provide a world-class cricketing experience for players of all skill levels.

Whether you're playing a friendly match, hosting a corporate event, or competing in a tournament, Sky Box offers top-quality turf, state-of-the-art floodlights, and professional amenities.

Join us and elevate your game in a vibrant, community-driven environment!
